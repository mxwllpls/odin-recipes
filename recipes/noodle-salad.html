Noodle Salad

This salad is a fresh summer dish that is packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

Salad Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of noodles
  • 1/2 a head of sliced napa cabbage
  • 1/2 sliced purple cabbage
  • 1/2 bag of baby spinach
  • 1 whole red bell pepper
  • 1 whole yellow bell pepper
  • 1 whole orange bell pepper
  • 1 bag bean sprouts
  • Chopped cilantro to taste
  • 3 whole scallions
  • 3 whole cucumbers
  • 10 oz whole cashews

Dressing Ingredients:

  • 1 whole lime, juiced
  • 8 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2-3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons fresh ginger, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 2 whole hot peppers, chopped
  • Chopped cilantro, to taste

Directions:

  1. >Mix salad ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk together dressing ingredients and pour over salad.
  3. Mix with tongs or hands and serve.
